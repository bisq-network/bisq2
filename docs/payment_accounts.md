# Payment accounts

## ACH Transfer

**Availability:** United States only
**Currency:** US dollars (USD)
**Overview:** Standard domestic bank transfer used widely for everyday payments in the US.
**Chargeback risk:** **LOW**

**Required input:** Routing number, account number
**Optional data:** None
